Empowering Future Leaders with Quality Education

Cavendish University Uganda (CUU) is a premier private university dedicated to providing high-quality, student-centered education. With a focus on modern learning approaches, industry-driven programs, and a diverse student community, CUU nurtures leadership, creativity, and critical thinking.

About CUU

Mission: Transforming students into responsible, educated, and skilled professionals through innovative education.

Vision: To be a model university that delivers quality education and produces graduates who positively impact their communities and the world.

History:

  • 2008: CUU was established as a private university in Uganda.
  • 2010: The university introduced online and blended learning programs.
